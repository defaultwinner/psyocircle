PSY 'O' CIRCLE

Psy 'O' Circle is a Delhi based band that plays Rock n Roll and Classic Old School Metal plus all the bluesy feeling. The band Jams at MGMH Sound Station, South Delhi.

Keeping the idea of the Classic era of late 1960's and 70's, the band plays around to experiment with different sounds and effects based on Guitar and Vocals, the bass and drums to come forth with something they don't know yet.

The band currently consists of 4 members-
  • Vinit (Vocals)
  • Sarthak (Guitars)
  • Vinit (Vocals)
  • Vinit (Vocals)
Here every individual in the unit has been touched by very different genres of this magical legacy of rock n roll. Yet when they come together they feel the need to move past their mp3 playlists because there are more to foster.
Currently Psy 'O" Circle jams at MGMH Sound Station, Okhla, South Delhi and is working on the compositions for the debut EP.
Stay tuned for the updates Guys, this is just the start of this unit!!! Let's get the Rock n Roll' out of its Grave and add some gunpowder to it.
